About us


We are three students of IES Zubiri Manteo, studying a higher level of Network System Administrations. In this case, we are working on a project about different Map API's. So we started working with Google Maps and Leaflet.




What is Leaflet?

Leaflet is the leading open-source JavaScript library for mobile-friendly interactive maps. Weighing just about 38 KB of JS, it has all the mapping features most developers ever need.
